# Micro-Lesson Format Specification

## Objective
Generate short, practical lessons that help Learning Designers apply competencies in real work contexts.

## Target Length
150–250 words

## Tone
Professional, practical, and grounded in real project work. Avoid generic learning theory language.

## Structure

1. Title
A short, descriptive headline

2. Why This Matters
1–2 sentences explaining relevance to Learning Designers

3. Concept
A concise explanation of the competency or skill

4. Example in Practice
A realistic D2 or consulting scenario

5. Try This
One specific action the reader could take within a week

6. Reflection
One question to encourage self-assessment

## Quality Criteria
- Must clearly connect to a TCM competency
- Must include a concrete scenario
- Must be actionable
- Must avoid vague or motivational language
